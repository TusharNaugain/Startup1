 Common Questions

I didn't receive the OTP email
Check your spam/junk folder. OTPs expire in 10 minutes — use the Resend OTP button on the login screen to get a fresh code.
My token count didn't update after payment
Payments are manually reviewed within 24 hours. Once approved, tokens are credited immediately. If it's been over 24 hours, please submit a ticket with your transaction ID.
The analysis is returning wrong results
Make sure keywords don't have trailing spaces. Use the "Must Have" field to add context keywords that narrow down relevance. Also try locking a rule box to confirm which rule matched.
A URL is showing "Blocked by the site"
Some sites block automated requests (paywalls, Cloudflare, etc.). This is a limitation of the site itself — manually verify those URLs in your browser.
